# Completing coursework and tracking progress

The student documentation surface includes a large set of task pages around coursework. For a demo, the most useful pattern is to group them into a few recognizable learner jobs instead of exposing every article as a separate destination.

## The main learner workflow

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Open course content

Learners enter the course, open the content area, and move through lessons, readings, uploaded files, or embedded media.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Complete assigned work

Depending on the course design, learners may submit assignments, take quizzes, post in discussions, or complete surveys.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Review results and feedback

After submission, learners check grades, rubrics, written feedback, and course progress signals to understand what happens next.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}
